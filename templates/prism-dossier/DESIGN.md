# Prism Dossier

Use for public researcher, lab, and project profile decks that should feel like a
polished academic homepage translated into slides.

## Visual Grammar

- Left identity/source rail, right research/content field.
- Serif display headings with quiet sans body text.
- Near-white surface, slate text, warm gold highlight.
- Publication/project rows and source artifacts are preferred over generic cards.
- Rounded elements stay modest; the polish comes from spacing, not decoration.

## Slide Jobs

- Cover: large identity rail plus concise positioning and credibility anchors.
- Content: claim bullets on the left, readable artifact panel on the right.
- Evidence: project/page/figure surface dominates the slide.
- Matrix: compact selected-work map, never a full CV dump.

## Avoid

- Purple gradients, fake dashboards, floating icon clusters, and portraits as
  proof.
- Empty title pages with tiny metadata.
- More than one decorative treatment per page.
