# Pentagram Gridnote

Purpose: bold method and systems decks that need typographic authority without
generic AI styling.

- Surface: cream or black field, hard black rules, one red signal color.
- Typography: heavy sans headline, compact sans body, monospace only for source
  labels and indices.
- Composition: poster covers, ledger proof pages, hard-bordered matrices, and
  large numbers treated as typographic anchors.
- Evidence: proof surfaces sit inside strict frames; annotation should be sparse
  and aligned to the grid.
- Cadence: use it as a deliberate reset for strong claims, not as a warm profile
  default.
- Avoid: neon, gradients, rounded cards, and fake dashboard decoration.
