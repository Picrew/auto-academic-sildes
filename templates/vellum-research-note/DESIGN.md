# Vellum Research Note

Use for formal seminar, thesis, research-note, or paper-defense decks that need
quiet authority.

## Reference DNA

- Beautiful HTML Templates: Vellum.
- Scholarly handouts, annotated notes, and dark library interfaces.
- Classic serif hierarchy with restrained gold signals.

## Grammar

- Deep navy vellum surface, warm serif type, muted gold accent.
- Best when the material is reflective, formal, or theory-heavy.
- Proof pages should still carry large readable evidence; do not let the dark
  mood turn screenshots into small ornaments.
- Cover images are identity anchors only unless explicitly enlarged.
- Avoid using it as a universal profile default; it can become too solemn.

## Best Fit

- Thesis defenses and seminars.
- Formal paper talks.
- Research-note decks with dense argument flow.
