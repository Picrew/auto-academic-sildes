# Broadsheet Data Room

Use for source-heavy profiles, benchmark comparisons, paper dossiers, and
technical reviews that should feel dense, sober, and authored.

## Grammar

- Off-white paper, black rules, and one citation-blue accent.
- Serif titles, compact sans/mono metadata, and scan-friendly ledgers.
- High fill is good when every row has evidence, comparison, or provenance.
- Use tables, marginal rails, and figure captions rather than decorative cards.
- Avoid gradient hero sections, empty white fields, and vague summary boxes.

## Layout

- Cover: `cover-source-rail`
- Evidence: `proof-atlas-spread`
- Artifact content: `artifact-marginalia`
- Matrix: `matrix-ledger`
- Labels: `content-marginalia`

## Image Rules

- Treat screenshots as figures in a serious report: crop tightly, caption
  specifically, and keep source pixels large.
- Use homepage/profile crops sparingly; prefer paper figures, repos, result
  charts, and benchmark surfaces.
- If a content slide is below useful fill, add source rows or convert to a
  matrix/ledger rather than enlarging decoration.
