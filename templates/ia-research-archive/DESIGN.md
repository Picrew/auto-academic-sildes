# IA Research Archive

Purpose: source-linked paper/profile decks that should feel like a serious
research archive or review.

- Surface: white page, black text, gray rules, single citation-blue accent.
- Typography: classic serif for headlines; plain sans or system font for dense
  body text and metadata.
- Composition: marginal rails, proof plate plus note column, ledger matrices,
  source rows instead of feature cards.
- Evidence: every image crop should explain what the viewer is inspecting; full
  webpages are allowed only as provenance anchors.
- Cadence: text-first pages can be dense, but proof pages must give the artifact
  at least half the slide.
- Avoid: marketing hero layouts, decorative screenshots, glow, glass, and
  unsourced summary boxes.
