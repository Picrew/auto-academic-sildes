# Mono Ink Ledger

Use for source-linked dossiers, handouts, literature reviews, and printed-review
style decks.

## Reference DNA

- Beautiful HTML Templates: Monochrome.
- Academic ledgers, marginal notes, and publication indexes.
- Information Architects-style restraint.

## Grammar

- Ivory paper, black ink, no decorative color.
- Marginalia and atlas spreads are the primary compositions.
- Strong as a document-like special case; less ideal for projection when proof
  screenshots must dominate the slide.
- Treat `proof-image-small` warnings seriously: enlarge the crop or switch to a
  more evidence-forward grammar for talks.
- Avoid using it for demos or high-energy product presentations.

## Best Fit

- Handout-like research dossiers.
- Literature reviews and paper maps.
- Profile decks where source links matter more than stage drama.
