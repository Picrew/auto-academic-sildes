# Huashu Issue Broadsheet

Use this grammar when a technical profile, benchmark, or systems story needs a
bold issue-board voice without gradient gloss.

## Visual Grammar

- Cream paper, hard black rules, and one red signal accent.
- Dense typographic hierarchy with boxed metrics and ledger rows.
- Poster-grid covers; field-manual or ledger content pages; proof-ledger
  evidence pages.
- Images are treated as inspected artifacts inside hard frames, not decorations.
- Strong contrast is allowed, but every box needs a job: claim, axis, metric,
  proof, or source.

## Use

- Tool-use, benchmark, systems, and infrastructure decks.
- Research profiles where projects are more important than biography.
- Handout-like talks that should scan quickly in a room.

## Avoid

- Long metric values in boxed cells; convert them to bullets or matrix rows.
- Decorative black/red styling without evidence.
- More bullets, metrics, labels, or callouts than the renderer can show.
