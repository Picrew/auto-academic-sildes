# Swiss Systems

For systems, infrastructure, benchmark, API, and engineering decks.

## Feel

Precise, modular, operational. It should look like a field manual for complex
systems work.

## Layout

- grid-first composition
- comparison matrices
- route maps
- interface boundary diagrams
- leaderboards and tables cropped tightly

## Visual Choices

- white or near-white background
- black/graphite type
- one accent color only
- compact labels and strong alignment
- tables with visible hierarchy

## Avoid

- hero-style marketing visuals
- generic architecture blobs
- screenshots with unreadable labels
- too many card-like panels
