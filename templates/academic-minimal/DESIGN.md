# Academic Minimal

Use when the audience expects a formal research talk.

- Route: Beamer backup or restrained PPTX.
- Background: warm paper.
- Typography: serif headlines, small sans body, mono kickers.
- Layouts: thesis, evidence, process, close.
- Avoid: product-style screenshots unless they are primary evidence.
- Best for: paper talks, thesis defense, reading group.

Design rule: if a figure is not readable, rebuild it as a native chart or move it to appendix.
