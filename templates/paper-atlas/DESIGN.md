# Paper Atlas

For paper readings, surveys, conference talks, and literature review decks.

## Feel

Academic but not Beamer-stiff. It should help the audience inspect a paper's
argument.

## Layout

- claim-led cover
- problem pressure map
- method diagram
- one figure close-read per important result
- compact limitations and implications
- citation/source strip on evidence slides

## Visual Choices

- paper background
- serif section heads
- readable figure crops
- small citation notes
- generous margins around figures

## Avoid

- abstract pasted as bullets
- full-paper screenshots without crop
- tiny multi-panel figures
- generic "Motivation / Method / Results" titles
