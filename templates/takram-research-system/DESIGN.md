# Takram Research System

Purpose: calm research-system and product/technical decks that need precision,
quiet density, and thoughtful hierarchy.

- Surface: soft off-white, pale system panels, charcoal ink, muted jade signal.
- Typography: restrained sans headline, compact body, thin metadata.
- Composition: source rail covers, proof-first atlas spreads, thin-rule ledgers,
  and clean artifact dossiers.
- Evidence: artifacts are specimens; crop away browser chrome and keep source
  labels quiet.
- Cadence: alternate calm system pages with proof plates so the deck feels
  deliberate rather than templated.
- Avoid: ornamental illustration, color washes, random icons, and nested cards.
