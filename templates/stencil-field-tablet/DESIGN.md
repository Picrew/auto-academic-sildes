# Stencil Field Tablet

Source influence: Beautiful HTML Templates `stencil-tablet`, archival field
manuals, municipal stencil signage, and tactile project surveys.

Use when a project ecosystem, technical survey, or field report needs more color
and tactile presence than PRISM, while staying disciplined enough for academic
content.

## Visual Grammar

- Bone paper, black stencil-like display type, and colored tablet modules.
- Color must encode category, status, method, source family, or evidence role.
- Headlines are poster-like; body and captions stay compact and legible.
- Artifact slides should read like field specimens with labels, not product
  marketing cards.
- Avoid playful sticker energy unless the source material itself is informal.

## Layout Defaults

- Cover: `cover-poster-grid`
- Proof: `proof-ledger`
- Artifact: `artifact-showcase`
- Matrix: `matrix-ledger`
- Content with labels: `content-field-manual`

## Evidence Rule

Use colored tablets to make source classes scan faster, not to fill empty space.
Source-heavy images still need the same artifact-size and rendered-pixel gates
as sober academic grammars.
