# Editorial Profile

For public researcher, lab, company, or portfolio profile decks.

## Feel

Quiet, exact, mature. It should read like a careful research briefing, not a
LinkedIn carousel or resume template.

## Layout

- cover: large name and claim, small source surface
- signature slide: short thesis and 3-4 precise bullets
- contribution map: table or matrix, not a timeline dump
- evidence slides: homepage/profile and representative artifact
- close: public synthesis, not internal advice

## Visual Choices

- warm paper and deep graphite
- serif headline, restrained sans body, occasional mono labels
- small gold/copper accents
- thin rules and strict alignment
- no portraits as hero images unless identity is the topic

## Avoid

- "A strong X deck should..." final slides
- inflated claims from public snippets
- decorative GitHub screenshots
- repeated phrases across many people
