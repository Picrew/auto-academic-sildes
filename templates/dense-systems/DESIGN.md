# Dense Systems

Use when the deck must explain infrastructure, model pipelines, or production loops.

- Route: PPTX-native, optionally with Beamer handout.
- Background: warm paper for explanation, graphite for taxonomy or summary.
- Typography: compact but readable; do not let body copy become footnote texture.
- Visuals: native diagrams over screenshots when architecture images clash with the deck style.
- Layouts: system, loop, matrix, process.
- Avoid: shrinking full architecture diagrams until labels disappear.

Design rule: show failure propagation and control points, not every component.
