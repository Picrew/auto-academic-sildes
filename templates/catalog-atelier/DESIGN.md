# Catalog Atelier

Use for people, project, and lab decks that should feel curated and tactile:
warm, research-oriented, and richer than a plain academic homepage.

## Sources

- Huashu design-styles: Stamen/Fathom/Takram attitude toward information as
  material, not decoration.
- Beautiful HTML templates: editorial catalog rhythm, warm paper, selected
  artifact panels, restrained color.
- Academic homepage practice: keep identity and publications source-linked.

## Visual Grammar

- Warm paper, deep green-black, sage/terracotta accents.
- Serif headings with catalog-style captions and thin rules.
- Proof surfaces should feel selected and cropped, like exhibition plates.
- Alternates gallery-scale proof pages with compact index/ledger pages.
- Density target: 55-65% useful fill; leave breathing room around evidence, not
  around empty text.

## Slide Jobs

- Cover: title with a source surface, not a marketing hero.
- Content: claim plus curated artifact panel.
- Matrix: research-map ledger with short rows.
- Evidence: large proof plate and restrained captions.
- Close: catalog note that ties source artifacts back to the thesis.

## Avoid

- Beige-only sameness, decorative mood boards, stock photography.
- Too many equal cards; one selected artifact should lead each visual page.
- Screenshots without crop or caption.
