# Neo Grid Lab

Use for demos, AI products, hack/research prototypes, and engineering stories
that can carry a high-energy visual system.

## Reference DNA

- Beautiful HTML Templates: Neo-Grid Bold.
- Hard-grid product demos, experimental lab posters, and tool dashboards.
- High contrast used as structure, not decoration.

## Grammar

- Cream or black base, heavy grid, neon yellow signal.
- Poster-grid covers and ledger proof slides; typography is intentionally loud.
- Keep academic content short and evidence large, otherwise the style overpowers
  the argument.
- Use as a special-case reset, not a default conference-paper grammar.
- Avoid neon gradients, glows, and decorative AI motifs.

## Best Fit

- AI product demos and engineering showcases.
- Tooling, benchmark, or agent workflow presentations.
- Short talks where visual energy is part of the brief.
