# Dark Lab Notebook

For frontier AI, RL, agent traces, reasoning behavior, and experiment-heavy
research decks.

## Feel

Serious, experimental, and controlled. It should feel like a lab notebook refined
for a talk.

## Layout

- dark anchor slides
- trace or metric ledgers
- figure close-reading with pins
- compact mechanisms
- failure -> signal -> intervention -> verification loops

## Visual Choices

- deep navy/graphite background
- warm paper counter-slides
- mono labels for runs, policies, traces
- muted copper or acid green only as signal
- evidence images placed large enough to inspect

## Avoid

- neon sci-fi styling
- fake terminal decoration
- large abstract gradients
- dense screenshots without callouts
