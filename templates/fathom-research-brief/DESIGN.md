# Fathom Research Brief

Use for scientific, systems, benchmark, and evaluation talks that need high
information density without becoming a spreadsheet.

## Visual Grammar

- Scientific report calm: cool gray surface, navy text, teal accent.
- Quantitative details become designed ledgers and figure notes.
- Every pixel should carry evidence, comparison, or orientation.
- Data and screenshots are large, but captions stay restrained.

## Slide Jobs

- Cover: compact research memo with metrics as lab ledger.
- Content: one finding plus a source artifact or measurement surface.
- Evidence: figure/table/project screenshot with 1-3 short pins.
- Matrix: dense comparison table with enough breathing room to scan.

## Avoid

- Decorative glow, heroic dark mode, and ornamental line art.
- Full-page screenshots with unreadable text.
- Metrics without a source trail.
