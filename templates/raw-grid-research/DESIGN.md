# Raw Grid Research

Source influence: Beautiful HTML Templates `raw-grid`, neobrutalist editorial
web systems, and benchmark boards.

Use when a technical deck needs graphic confidence, high density, and a strong
anti-corporate voice without becoming generic AI spectacle.

## Visual Grammar

- White canvas, black 3px rules, system sans type, hard offset shadows, and a
  few muted pastel blocks.
- The grid is the decoration: every border must define reading order,
  comparison, source, or hierarchy.
- High fill is expected. Empty space should feel like tension, not like missing
  content.
- Metrics and matrices become hard boards; proof images sit in black-framed
  evidence blocks.
- Avoid fake HUDs, glowing panels, glassmorphism, and random AI iconography.

## Layout Defaults

- Cover: `cover-poster-grid`
- Proof: `proof-ledger`
- Artifact: `artifact-ledger`
- Matrix: `matrix-ledger`
- Content with labels: `content-field-manual`

## Evidence Rule

If a source artifact is not large enough to read inside the hard frame, split the
slide or replace the crop. Raw-grid confidence only works when the proof is
substantial.
