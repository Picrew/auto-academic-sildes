# Systems Field Manual

Use for infrastructure, benchmarks, tool-use systems, and engineering research
where the deck should feel operational and scan-friendly.

## Reference DNA

- Muller-Brockmann grid rigor.
- Huashu Build / Fathom: exact information design, dense ledgers.
- Technical field manuals: numbered routes, boundaries, procedures.

## Grammar

- Neutral field-paper surface with black grid and one orange signal color.
- Matrix and content pages use ledger rows rather than generic cards.
- Proof pages keep a compact note rail and a large inspectable artifact.
- Every box must be an axis, state, component, route, or evidence surface.
- Avoid soft shadows and presentation-theater lighting.

## Best Fit

- API/tool-use systems.
- Benchmark and evaluation decks.
- Systems, pipelines, infrastructure, and methods talks.
