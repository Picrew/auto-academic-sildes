# Tech Editorial

Use for portfolios, deep technical interviews, AI systems talks, and founder-style research demos.

- Route: PPTX-native default.
- Background: warm paper with dark editorial anchor slides.
- Typography: strong serif action titles, quiet sans body.
- Accents: copper for structure, teal/cobalt only for charts.
- Layouts: cover, map, split, system, loop, product, close.
- Avoid: glass cards, large logos, purple gradients, generic icon grids.

Design rule: every evidence-heavy slide gets one large proof artifact, two callouts, and one implication.
