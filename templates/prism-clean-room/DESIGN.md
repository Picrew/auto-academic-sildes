# Prism Clean Room

Purpose: high-density academic homepage decks that should feel precise, mature,
and source-linked rather than résumé-like.

- Surface: near-white page, white proof panels, slate ink, warm gold source cues.
- Typography: Georgia-like serif for claims and section headers; system sans for
  body, captions, and ledgers.
- Composition: identity/source rail on covers, dossier proof pages, compact
  publication/project rows, no ornamental cards.
- Evidence: screenshots are clean specimens with thin borders and compact source
  captions; proof pages need a readable artifact, not a logo.
- Cadence: calm profile pages, proof pages, and matrix pages should differ by
  structure rather than palette.
- Avoid: glossy shadows, pill tags as decoration, gradient backgrounds, and
  homepage screenshots reused as evidence for unrelated claims.
