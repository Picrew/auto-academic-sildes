# Lab Trace Ledger

Use when the source material is experimental, agentic, or trace-like, but should
avoid generic sci-fi styling.

## Reference DNA

- Fathom rigor on a dark lab page.
- Experimental Jetset type discipline without poster excess.
- Agent traces and RL logs as evidence surfaces.

## Grammar

- Dark graphite base, pale text, acid-lime signal accent.
- Use ledgers, trace rows, and compact labels; no neon gradient spectacle.
- Evidence remains real screenshots, tables, traces, or project pages.
- Dark pages are functional: they reset cadence and frame experimental systems.
- Keep metrics short and source captions visible.

## Best Fit

- Agent/RL/tool-use decks.
- Reasoning behavior, post-training, ablations, and benchmark traces.
