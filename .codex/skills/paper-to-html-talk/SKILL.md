# Paper To HTML Talk Skill

Use for paper reading, conference talks, seminars, and journal club decks.

## Workflow

1. Identify the paper's central claim, not just the topic.
2. Build a narrative skeleton: problem pressure, method move, key evidence, limitations, takeaway.
3. Choose 2-4 figures or tables as proof objects.
4. Crop or redraw figures until labels are readable in `html-contact-sheet.png`.
5. Export HTML-first; use Beamer only for formal handout backup.

## Slide Moves

- claim cover
- problem pressure map
- method mechanism diagram
- figure close-read
- comparison evidence wall
- ablation or limitation slide
- synthesis / implications

Avoid dumping abstract bullets. Every slide title should be a judgment.
